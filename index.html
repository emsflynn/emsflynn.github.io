About Me

As a social butterfly with a passion for meeting new people and learning new things about the world of analytics, I have found the happy medium to be a mix between human interaction and data analytics. Learning about the possibilities within the data analytics has been an exciting and engaging journey for me. I love learning new ways to code and create visualisations to better understand different datasets. I have never liked problem solving, but through coding I have found that I actually quite enjoy it and find the challenges exciting. My favourite language is Python and working with complex datasets is fun for me. I am also proficient in PowerBI and SQL. My career aspirations strongly point towards sales and marketing due to the social hands on aspect of these careers. With the knowledge I have learnt through data analytics, I know that when I do embark on my career journey that I will have a lot to bring to the company. I have a passion for learning and developing my skillset and have vowed to myself to implement my creativity and passion in any career that I decide to pursue.
